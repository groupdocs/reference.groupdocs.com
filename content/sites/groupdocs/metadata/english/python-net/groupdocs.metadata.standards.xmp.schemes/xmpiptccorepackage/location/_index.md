## location property


Gets or sets the location the content is focusing on.

### Remarks 


This location name could either be the name of a sublocation to a city or the name of a well known location
or (natural) monument outside a city.
In the sense of a sublocation to a city this element is at the fourth level of a top-down geographical hierarchy.
