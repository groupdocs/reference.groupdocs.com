## WordProcessingSaveOptions.OoxmlCompliance property

Optional OOXML compliance level for the signed document. When set, overrides the original document's compliance. When null (default), the loaded document's compliance is preserved. Only honoured for OOXML formats (Docx, Docm, Dotx, Dotm, FlatOpc and FlatOpc variants).
