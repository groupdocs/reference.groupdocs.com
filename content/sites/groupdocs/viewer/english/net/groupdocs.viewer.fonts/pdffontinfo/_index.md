## PdfFontInfo structure

Encapsulates metainfo and binary data of one font from the PDF document, loaded into the [`Viewer`](../../groupdocs.viewer/viewer) instance. This font is used in the document content and is embedded inside the document itself or is installed in the operating system, where the GroupDocs.Viewer is running.
