## z_order_position property


Gets the position of this [`PresentationBaseShape`](/watermark/python-net/groupdocs.watermark.contents.presentation/presentationbaseshape) in the z-order.

### Remarks 


A shape with greater z-order is always in front of a shape with a lower z-order.
