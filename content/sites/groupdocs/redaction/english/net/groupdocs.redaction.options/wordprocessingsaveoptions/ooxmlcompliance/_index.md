## WordprocessingSaveOptions.OoxmlCompliance property

Gets or sets the OOXML compliance level for word processing documents. If not specified, the compliance level of the original document is preserved. A higher compliance level cannot be downgraded directly to the lowest level. See [`WordProcessingComplianceLevel`](../../wordprocessingcompliancelevel).
